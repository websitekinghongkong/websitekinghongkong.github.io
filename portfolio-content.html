BFIT DANCE

Overview

BFit Dance Studio is an energetic and colourful dance studio in Tsim Sha Tsui, opened by a group of passionate Filipino dancers.

With the use of high-contrast colours and bold web animation, our team crafted a unique digital experience for users to “dance” with site, while keeping a friendly browsing UI/UX.

This web design for BFit Dance Studio earned our team the Special Kudos Award from the CSS Design Awards community in 2022.
